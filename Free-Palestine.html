Palestine Needs To Be Free!




Violence has escalated in Palestine after months of ongoing tension across the region. In Gaza specifically, over six thousand people — including children — have been killed, thousands more have been injured, and hundreds of thousands have been forced to flee. A ‘total blockade’ is in place, completely cutting off two million residents from access to food, electricity, and fuel. And while some aid trucks were able to cross in from Egypt on Oct. 21, it is no where near enough, and many of the most-needed items were still not allowed in.

Civilians are paying a horrific price for the escalation of hostilities between Israeli forces and Palestinian armed groups. Some 2.1 million people in the Gaza Strip and the West Bank need urgent humanitarian aid and protection. You can help families caught up in this catastrophic situation.
